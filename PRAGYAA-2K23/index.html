About Us :

PRAGYAA is a national-level technical fiesta organized every year by SGGSIE&T, Nanded, to provide a platform for students to showcase their excellence in techno-social competitions. Each year, we work on a constructive theme focused on the welfare of society and technology. Students participate in large numbers in a multitude of events, ranging from brainstorming for junior scientists to bewildering robotics, and get a chance to delve into a variety of exhibits. Our aim is to focus on excellence in technology for human welfare and add a new dimension to the success of our extravaganza every year, so that it will reach the height of its glory.
